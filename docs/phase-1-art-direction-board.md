# Phase 1 Art Direction Board

## Core thesis

The site should feel like entering a dormant archive chamber inside a ruined museum complex:

- dark fantasy in atmosphere, not in IP borrowing
- museum curation in structure, not SaaS product cards
- one scarce ember light, not orange glow flooding
- stillness, stone, dust, relief, and catalog language over spectacle

## Image direction

- Main hero plate: one architectural threshold image with deep arch framing and a distant tower read
- Secondary motifs: guardian remnant, dragon trace, manuscript surface, armor details
- Texture support: black stone, oxidized metal, smoke, ember, worn parchment
- No collage wall of references on the live page

## Hero composition rules

- Left column: title, role statement, short body, one primary CTA, one muted secondary CTA
- Right column: a single “display plate” scene, treated like a curated exhibit window
- Required anchors inside the scene:
  - ruined or ancient threshold
  - guardian remnant silhouette
  - distant tower or battlement silhouette
  - faint dragon trace
  - one controlled firelight source

## Design system guardrails

- Palette:
  - soot black
  - weathered stone gray
  - bone text
  - muted brass
  - one ember orange
- Typography:
  - display serif for monument and archive tone
  - restrained sans for labels and body
- Borders:
  - etched lines, top and bottom rules, plate framing
  - avoid soft floating cards
- Motion:
  - scene drift, ember pulse, minor parallax only
  - opacity and transform only
  - reduced-motion mode removes the drift and pulse

## Do / avoid

- Do make the first screen read as a location
- Do make the project archive feel curated and deliberate
- Do keep contrast strong and highlight use scarce
- Avoid game poster framing
- Avoid bright fantasy matte-painting color
- Avoid full-screen fog layers
- Avoid pill-heavy SaaS UI language
