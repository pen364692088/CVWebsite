# Phase 1 Material Research

This table narrows the visual source pool to assets that fit the `Dark Fantasy + Museum Curation Portfolio` direction without using game IP, fan edits, Pinterest collage material, or third-party concept art.

## License baselines

- Pexels: the Pexels license allows use, modification, and commercial use; attribution is not required.
- The Met Open Access: public-domain images are available under CC0 with unrestricted reuse.

## Selection notes

- Phase 1 hero uses a single main image only: `Norwich Cathedral behind Woman Walking in Tunnel`.
- `Column Statue of a King` and the dragon drawing are reference-safe anchors for silhouette and relief language; they are not required to appear as full photographic plates in the hero.
- Any future use of Pexels assets in the final UI should still be tracked with page URLs in this document so provenance stays clear.
